2. Items (needs images)

  • Display within selected category
  • Clear, vibrant item images (banana image for banana, egg image for egg)
  • Add/remove buttons

3. My List

  • Checkboxes
  • Remove button per item
  • Show which user/child added each item (avatar)
  • Quantity adjustment per item

4. Shop Mode

  • Simulation mode
  • Check off items as "shopped"

5. Recipes (enhanced)

  • Recipe cards
  • +/- buttons to adjust ingredient quantities
  • Uncheck individual ingredients (e.g., "unshack shells if already have")
  • Substitution voting: "We have X, could make Y. Vote?"

6. Budget Challenge (enhanced)

  • Editable budget amount (not fixed at $20)
  • Tracks spent
  • Teaching tool — prices don't need to be exact
  • Parent checkout — mom decides if expensive items are actually purchased
  • Live pricing could come later

7. Family Hub NEW

  • Master list of all items from all family members
  • Sort by: user, category, store, recipe
  • Voting visualization: show avatars for items, e.g., "ice cream" shows 3 kids' avatars, "oreos" shows 1
  • Store preferences (optional): assign items to Store A, B, etc.
  • Sort by store before export (free alternative to AnyList paid tier)

8. Inventory NEW

  • Track all food currently in household
  • Auto-suggest recipes based on what we have
  • Restocking reminders

9. House Potluck NEW

  • Assign parts of a meal to different family members
  • Example: "mashed potatoes to Michael, protein to Mom, cold sides to Kid A"

10. Parent Dashboard (enhanced)

  • View all family lists
  • Age-based requirements: e.g., "Child 2 must pick 1 fruit, 1 veggie, 1 other side per week"
  • Activity tracking: show what's "not completed"
  • Themed activities: "shape of the week," "color of the week"
  • Export list (rename from "Export to AnyList" → "Export List")
